## Dataset Source
Kaggle - Churn Modelling Dataset
Source:https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/savitanair/curn-modelling

## Problem Statement
Predict whether a customer will exit the bank based on demographic and financial features.

## Business Relevance
Helps banks identify at-risk customers and improve retention strategies.

## Key Insights
- Older customers are more likely to churn.
- Inactive members show higher churn rates.
- The dataset has moderate class imbalance (~20%).

## Preprocessing Summary
- Removed identifier columns
- No missing values
- No duplicate records
- Encoded categorical variables
- Applied feature scaling after train-test split
